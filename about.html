It's a club, but more than that — it's a way we feel sometimes.

Once you enter the club for the first time, you'll always be a member. Feelings can't be undone or unseen. Feelings are not a downside or a flaw — they are your power. They mean you can see and experience the world in a way that many others can't. No matter how sucky it can be at times.

You and I, we've seen things, felt things, and somehow found our way back. We're here, living this complicated life the best we can.
I applaud you, brave soul, for being here — for pushing your way through life.

— Sad Sunday Club
