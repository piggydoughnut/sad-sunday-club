About the artist behind the club.

Hi! I am Daria, I draw things that come to me on random occasions, and quite often it is on Sundays.

It all started as a feeling I couldn't quite name on a Sunday afternoon. The weekend was already half gone and Monday was knocking at the door with the alarms, the inbox, the whole machinery of the week just around the corner.

I find Sundays to be good for contemplation. Not every Sunday is the same but at least once a month I find myself thinking about things.

Those Sunday feelings are not quite sadness, not quite peace. They are a particular kind of quiet where the thoughts you've been too busy to hear all week start to surface.

I always wanted to share my art with the world, but could never quite find the right channel. I tried going the instagram route, but it felt too public, too loud and too competitive. My art is not trying to better than any other art, it just wants to exist. So here we are. This newsletter is a small, handmade artifact of those Sunday hours. Reflections, observations, thoughts that deserve somewhere to go.

Welcome to the club.

— Daria from Sad Sunday Club
