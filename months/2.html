About coffee

Coffee? Mmmm. Tasty? Not really. It's bitter, and sometimes even sour. For some completely unknown reason everyone is obsessed with coffee (me included).

When I was a little kid I didn't understand why people like coffee as it does not actually taste good. Same went with alcohol. It was a complete mystery to me. I even made a pact with my friend in the 4th grade that we will never drink alcohol or coffee as both seem completely yuck and silly.

Fast-forward about 20 years, and now I'm the person chugging coffee like there is no tomorrow, out of what might as well be a 5L jar.

I drink half a liter in the morning. Followed by a flat white from a coffee shop across the road, and a few cups of black tea with milk like I am some sort of English person. So I am most certainly jacked up to the max all day every day.

Sad Sunday Club, made by a human, for humans.
