About

Some days I just want to crawl under the bed, like not under the blanket or covers, but just under the bed. I want to sit there with snacks and maybe a book. Other days I just want to be down there and cry all day. It really all depends on the day.

I have never actually performmed the act of crawling under the bed, as it might be a little hard being a full grown human to manage that. Also my bed does not really have that imaginary under the bed sanctuary. But if it did, trust me I would have made it my own little office down there.

Just think about it, no one bothers you when you hang out under your bed. Well they simiply can't see you. It's a quiet place to be. This brings me to a thought - do you clean under your bed? Maybe it is time to dust it off down there. You never know when you might need it.

Loads of love,
yours truly,
